Methodology

The composite score blends volume (commit count + code churn), consistency (active days and cadence over tenure), and quality (commit-message discipline and low-effort ratio){% if llm %}, plus model-judged quality (craftsmanship and authenticity, weighted 60/40){% endif %}. Scores are shrunk toward zero for small samples (a credibility factor of commits ÷ (commits + 8)) so a single drive-by commit can't out-rank sustained work.
